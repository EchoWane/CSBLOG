# Testing KaTeX Math Rendering

## Inline Math

Here's some inline math: $E = mc^2$ and another example: $\int_0^\infty e^{-x^2} dx = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}$

The Pythagorean theorem states that $a^2 + b^2 = c^2$ for a right triangle.

## Block Math

Here's a block equation:

$$
\frac{d}{dx}\left( \int_{0}^{x} f(u)\,du\right)=f(x)
$$

Another example with matrices:

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
x \\
y
\end{bmatrix}
=
\begin{bmatrix}
ax + by \\
cx + dy
\end{bmatrix}
$$

The quadratic formula:

$$
x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}
$$

## More Complex Examples

The Fourier Transform:

$$
\hat{f}(\xi) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(x)\ e^{-2\pi i x \xi}\,dx
$$

Euler's formula: $e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0$

Greek letters: $\alpha, \beta, \gamma, \Delta, \Omega$

If you can see properly formatted mathematical equations above, KaTeX is working correctly!
